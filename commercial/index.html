The Water

Three serves. One system.

01 - Still

Chilled Still

Multi-stage filtration removes chlorine, sediment, heavy metals and off-tastes, then a mineral profile is restored for a clean, soft finish. Served ice-cold, straight from the font.

02 - Sparkling

Fine Sparkling

Food-grade CO₂ dissolved under pressure into chilled, remineralised water for a fine, persistent bead of sparkling mineral water.

03 - Ambient

Ambient Filtered

The Systems

Suited to your demand.
Above expectation.

A compact under-counter plant paired with an elegant counter font or tap - sized to your covers, installed by our technicians, and maintained on a scheduled service programme.

Step 01

Filter

Reverse osmosis filtration polishes the water, removing contaminants found in our tap water - sediment, chlorine, fluoride, nitrates, taste & odour.

Step 02

Remineralise

A geologist-formulated blend of natural mineral rock restores calcium, magnesium and trace elements the way nature does - recreating the taste and balance of spring water, straight from the tap.

Step 03

Chill & Carbonate

High-capacity chilling and inline carbonation deliver consistent temperature and bead through peak service.

Step 04

Serve

Dispensed from a premium font into your own premium reusable glass bottles - endlessly presentable.
