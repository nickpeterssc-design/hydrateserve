About us

Water is the driving force of all nature.

HydrateServe is the premium water tap of Hydrate Filters, New Zealand's filtration specialists. We've spent years perfecting water in Kiwi homes. Now we put it on tap - filtered, chilled, sparkling and boiling - for kitchens and venues across the country.

Our Story

From city pipes to natural mineral balance.

Seeing the reality of ageing water infrastructure in New Zealand and around the world sparked a simple question for Nic: there's got to be a better way to drink water every day?

That question became an obsession. Years were spent researching, testing and refining different filtration technologies - to find solutions that genuinely improved water quality without compromising what makes water healthy.

HydrateServe takes that same obsession further, and is the natural route from here: our leading filtration systems, paired with options for boiling, chilled & sparkling. Quality, certified systems - with leading technology and end usability a priority.

What We Stand For

Three things we won't compromise.

01

Health before everything

Filtration is paramount. We see so many sub-par systems out there, and unfortunately NZ water quality is not improving - the key for us is accurately filtered water. Specs matter here.

02

Nothing trucked, nothing wasted

Let's protect our planet. Premium water shouldn't cross an ocean to reach a kitchen in New Zealand. Everything we install makes it on site - no freight, no single-use packaging, no empties.

03

We're passionate and dedicated

A water system is only premium while it's performing. Scheduled servicing, filter changes and CO₂ supply are part of every installation - you pour, we look after the rest.
